About Muthoni RN

I'm a registered nurse based in Kenya, dedicated to transforming lives through quality healthcare. My journey in nursing began with a deep desire to make a difference in my community, especially for those who find it challenging to access traditional healthcare settings. Over the years, I've worked with diverse patients, gaining expertise in home-based care, elderly support, and chronic illness management.

I'm proud to contribute to Kenya's growing focus on community health, a movement that empowers families to receive care in their homes while promoting independence and dignity. My training and experience align with the standards set by global nursing organizations like the American Nurses Association, which champions excellence in nursing care.

When I'm not caring for patients, I enjoy connecting with my community, sharing health education, and spending time outdoors – as you can see from my photo!
